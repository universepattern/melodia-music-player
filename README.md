# Melodia Music Player

**Live Demo:** [https://universepattern.github.io/melodia-music-player/](https://universepattern.github.io/melodia-music-player/)

A beautiful, cozy, browser-based local music player. Drop your files or folders and enjoy your music with a rich, dark, and warm aesthetic featuring a fluid oil-paint visualizer.

## Features

- **Cozy Dark Aesthetic**: A stunning glassmorphic UI with warm orange and gold accents.
- **Drag & Drop Playback**: Easily play your local `.mp3`, `.flac`, `.wav`, and other audio files by dragging and dropping them into the browser.
- **Folder Support**: Drop an entire folder to instantly queue up all your music.
- **ID3 Tag Parsing**: Automatically extracts and displays Track Title, Artist, and Album Cover Art from your audio files.
- **Audio Visualizer**: A custom fluid oil-paint canvas animation that reacts to the bass, mids, and highs of the currently playing track, gracefully blended over your album cover.
- **Interactive Waveform**: Seek through your music visually with the dynamic waveform timeline.
- **Queue Management**: Clear, organized sidebar displaying your upcoming tracks with active playing indicators.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Space to play/pause, Arrows to seek/volume, N/P for next/previous.
